Best QuickBooks Online alternatives for 2026

Independent picks. Affiliate links noted.

QuickBooks Online is a powerful workhorse, but it can feel like overkill if your business outgrows its complexity or your monthly costs creep up without clear added value. If you find yourself paying for features you rarely use, or if your accountant is pushing for a more collaborative platform, it might be time to explore alternatives that align better with your actual workflow.

The best alternatives to QuickBooks Online

  • Xero
    Xero is a top choice for growing businesses and accountant-led teams that want a modern, collaborative platform. It frequently earns praise for its clean interface and strong multi-user access, making it easier for you and your accountant to work in real time.
    Visit site: Xero →
  • FreshBooks
    FreshBooks shines for freelancers, consultants, and service-based small businesses that invoice clients regularly. Users commonly report that its time tracking and expense management are intuitive, and its mobile app is a standout for on-the-go billing.
    Visit site: FreshBooks →
  • Wave
    Wave is ideal for solo founders, freelancers, and very small businesses that need solid accounting basics without a subscription. It is widely adopted by budget-conscious teams because its core features are free, with optional paid add-ons for payments and payroll.
    Visit site: Wave →
  • Zoho Books
    Zoho Books is a strong fit for small to mid-size businesses already using Zoho tools, or teams wanting strong automation at a competitive price. It is frequently cited for its seamless integration with the Zoho ecosystem and its ability to automate repetitive tasks like bank reconciliation and invoice reminders.
